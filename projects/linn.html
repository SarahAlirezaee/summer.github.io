LINN: Path of Orchards

About Linn

Linn is a premium 2D modern puzzle platformer with a unique art style which tries to give player a precious experience of discovering a mystical ancient dynamic environment in a ​touch-based game. With a ​swipe​ of finger character can jump, dash and swiftly move through air.
